About the Game

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. Released in 2018, it takes place in a brutal and fading American frontier, where lawlessness still rules the land. You play as Arthur Morgan, an outlaw forced to survive as the Wild West collapses around him. With cinematic storytelling, unmatched realism, and a living world, the game delivers one of the most immersive experiences ever created.
